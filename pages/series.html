WandaVision (2021)

Wanda Maximoff and Vision live an idyllic suburban life until reality starts to crack. A mind-bending blend of sitcoms and superhero mystery, this series explores grief, trauma, and Wanda’s transformation into the Scarlet Witch, setting up future chaos in the multiverse.

The Falcon and the Winter Soldier (2021)

Sam Wilson and Bucky Barnes team up to stop a global threat post-Endgame. As Sam grapples with taking up Captain America’s shield, the show explores legacy, race, and identity with grounded action and a deep dive into modern geopolitics.

Loki Season 1 (2021)

After stealing the Tesseract, Loki finds himself in the custody of the mysterious TVA. As he uncovers their secrets, he confronts alternate versions of himself and a greater threat that could unravel time itself. It's quirky, cerebral, and sets up the multiverse saga.

What If... Season 1 (2021)

This animated anthology reimagines pivotal moments from the MCU in alternate timelines. From Peggy Carter becoming Captain Carter to zombie Avengers, each episode explores how one small change creates a whole new universe shaping possibilities for the multiverse’s future.

Hawkeye (2021)

Clint Barton teams up with eager archer Kate Bishop during the holidays. As old enemies resurface, Clint faces his past as Ronin. This grounded, action-packed series brings humor, heart, and the introduction of new street-level heroes, including Maya Lopez and Kingpin.

Moon Knight (2022)

Steven Grant, a mild-mannered gift shop worker, suffers blackouts and strange visions discovering he shares a body with mercenary Marc Spector, avatar of the Egyptian god Khonshu. This gripping psychological thriller explores identity, mental illness, mythology, and inner conflict, blending action and mystery with a mythological twist.

Ms. Marvel (2022)

Kamala Khan, a Pakistani-American teen and Avengers superfan, discovers cosmic powers linked to her heritage. Balancing high school life, family, and newfound heroism, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery. Colorful, fun, and deeply rooted in culture, the series introduces a fresh generation of heroes.

I Am Groot Season 1

In this adorable collection of animated shorts, Baby Groot embarks on mischievous mini-adventures across the galaxy. With no dialogue beyond “I am Groot,” these episodes are packed with charm, humor, and heart offering light-hearted, bite-sized Marvel fun between bigger stories.

She-Hulk: Attorney at Law (2022)

Jennifer Walters, cousin of Bruce Banner, gains Hulk powers and juggles her career as a lawyer in a superhuman world. Mixing legal comedy with fourth-wall-breaking meta-humor, it dives into identity, dating, and the absurdity of hero life with surprise cameos from across the MCU.

Werewolf by Night (2022)

In a retro-style horror tribute, monster hunters gather to compete for a powerful relic, unveiling secrets and horrors in the process. Jack Russell hides a monstrous alter ego. With gothic visuals and supernatural flair, it introduces Marvel’s darker, mystical side in under an hour.

The Guardians of the Galaxy Holiday Special (2022)

Determined to give Peter Quill a perfect Christmas, Drax and Mantis head to Earth and kidnap Kevin Bacon. This heartfelt, hilarious holiday short delivers music, laughs, and emotional beats bridging the Guardians' adventures between Vol. 2 and Vol. 3.

Secret Invasion (2023)

Nick Fury uncovers a covert Skrull infiltration of Earth’s leadership. With paranoia spreading and old alliances tested, this espionage thriller explores trust, identity, and betrayal in a darker, grounded MCU story. A spiritual successor to Captain America: The Winter Soldier, but with shapeshifters.

Loki Season 2 (2023)

Loki returns with Mobius and Sylvie to fix time’s unraveling chaos. As timelines collapse, Loki evolves from villain to selfless hero. Visually rich, emotionally resonant, and loaded with multiversal consequences, Season 2 deepens the MCU's time travel lore while teasing future threats.

What If...? Season 2 (2023)

The Watcher returns to guide us through more alternate realities in the multiverse. From a steampunk Earth to unexpected team-ups, the anthology deepens the “what if” premise with clever twists and emotional depth highlighting the infinite possibilities of the MCU.

Echo (2024)

Maya Lopez, introduced in Hawkeye, returns to her hometown and confronts her roots, trauma, and Kingpin. This grounded, gritty series blends action with cultural identity, family, and redemption becoming Marvel’s first series to spotlight a deaf Native American hero with intense street-level storytelling.

Agatha: Darkhold Diaries (2024)

After the events of WandaVision, Agatha Harkness is back with a new mission. Trapped in Westview or on the run, this series delves into her magical powers, backstory, and dark ambitions. With chaos brewing, Agatha’s journey through the mystical and dangerous corners of the MCU is sure to be full of twists.

Daredevil: Born Again (2024)

Matt Murdock, aka Daredevil, returns to the MCU in this 18-episode series. Rebuilding his life as a lawyer and vigilante, Daredevil confronts new threats, both legal and criminal, in Hell's Kitchen. This darker, grittier series embraces the character's roots while exploring his role in a larger, connected universe.
