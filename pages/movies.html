Phase 1

Iron Man (2008)

A genius billionaire, Tony Stark, is kidnapped by terrorists. Using his intellect, he builds a suit to escape, becoming Iron Man. With his new-found armor, he confronts dangers while facing personal conflicts, ultimately taking on the responsibility of becoming a hero and saving the world.

The Incredible Hulk (2008)

Bruce Banner, a scientist on the run, struggles to control the Hulk, an uncontrollable alter-ego created by an experiment gone wrong. Pursued by the government, he seeks a cure, but his transformation brings him into conflict with another monstrous foe, leading to a battle for survival. He must choose between living in hiding or embracing the beast within to protect those around him.

Iron Man 2 (2010)

Tony Stark faces pressure from the government to share his Iron Man technology. His personal life unravels as new enemies, including Ivan Vanko (Whiplash), rise to challenge him. Stark struggles with his own mortality, while trying to defend his legacy and protect those he loves.

Thor (2011)

Banished from Asgard by his father Odin, Thor, the Norse god of thunder, must prove himself worthy. Stranded on Earth, he learns humility and discovers his true strength, protecting both Earth and Asgard from a dark force, Loki, who seeks to take over the Nine Realms. Thor’s journey sparks a powerful transformation from arrogance to heroism.

Captain America: The First Avenger (2011)

Steve Rogers, a frail young man, is transformed into the super-soldier Captain America during World War II. He faces the evil Hydra organization led by Red Skull. With his enhanced strength, Cap leads the charge against enemies, becoming a symbol of hope and freedom for the world.

The Avengers (2012)

Loki, the adopted brother of Thor, leads an alien invasion against Earth. Nick Fury, the head of S.H.I.E.L.D., assembles a team of superheroes Iron Man, Thor, Captain America, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye to stop the invasion. Together, they fight to protect Earth from destruction in an epic showdown.

Phase 2

Iron Man 3 (2013)

Tony Stark deals with the aftermath of the alien invasion in New York, struggling with anxiety and self-doubt. When a terrorist, the Mandarin, attacks him, Tony must rely on his intellect and his suits to stop him. His journey is one of personal growth and redemption. He learns that the true source of his strength lies beyond the armor.

Thor: The Dark World (2013)

Thor returns to battle the Dark Elf Malekith, who threatens to use the Aether to destroy the Nine Realms. As Thor struggles to save his home, he forms an uneasy alliance with Loki to prevent a universe-ending catastrophe, revealing hidden secrets about his family and Asgard's past.

Captain America: The Winter Soldier (2014)

Captain America uncovers a conspiracy within S.H.I.E.L.D. as he battles against the mysterious assassin, the Winter Soldier, who has ties to his past. As secrets unfold, Steve Rogers faces a moral dilemma, with his ideals and trust in his allies challenged by the shadowy forces within the government.

Guardians of the Galaxy (2014)

Peter Quill, a space outlaw, forms an unlikely team of misfits a talking raccoon, a walking tree, a vengeance seeking assassin, and a warrior princess. Together, they must stop a powerful villain, Ronan, from using an ancient artifact, the Orb, to destroy the galaxy, finding friendship along the way.

Avengers: Age of Ultron (2015)

Tony Stark creates Ultron, an artificial intelligence designed to protect Earth. However, Ultron turns rogue, seeking to destroy humanity. The Avengers must reunite to stop him and his army of robots. Meanwhile, tensions rise within the team, leading to conflict, personal challenges, and the introduction of new heroes.

Ant-Man (2015)

Scott Lang, a master thief, is recruited by Hank Pym to become the new Ant-Man. Equipped with a suit that allows him to shrink in size but increase in strength, Scott must stop Pym’s former protégé, Darren Cross, from selling a dangerous weapon. It’s a heist with heroic consequences.

Phase 3

Captain America: Civil War (2016)

The Avengers fracture after the Sokovia Accords, which require superheroes to be controlled by the government. Iron Man supports the Accords, while Captain America opposes them. This leads to a clash between the two heroes and their teams. The conflict grows personal, and new heroes are introduced.

Doctor Strange (2016)

Doctor Stephen Strange, a renowned neurosurgeon, is injured in a car accident and loses the use of his hands. Desperate for healing, he travels to Nepal and learns the mystic arts. As he becomes the Sorcerer Supreme, he must protect the Earth from interdimensional threats and dark magic.

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 (2017)

The Guardians face new challenges as they uncover the truth about Peter Quill’s parentage. As they battle powerful foes, including Quill’s estranged father, Ego, they learn more about their own identities and form a stronger bond as a family, all while facing cosmic threats and personal revelations.

Spider-Man: Homecoming (2017)

Peter Parker, a high school student, struggles with balancing his superhero life and normal teenage experiences. When the villainous Vulture emerges, Peter must prove himself as a hero without the full support of his mentor, Tony Stark, facing the challenges of being a young superhero in a vast world.

Thor: Ragnarok (2017)

Thor is imprisoned on a distant planet, where he must fight for survival in a gladiatorial arena against the Hulk. As the clock ticks, Thor races to stop his sister, Hela, from destroying Asgard and conquering the universe. It’s a journey of self-discovery, friendship, and cosmic-scale action.

Black Panther (2018)

Loki, the adopted brother of Thor, leads an alien invasion against Earth. Nick Fury, the head of S.H.I.E.L.D., assembles a team of superheroes Iron Man, Thor, Captain America, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye to stop the invasion. Together, they fight to protect Earth from destruction in an epic showdown.

Avengers: Infinity War (2018)

The Avengers and their allies unite to stop Thanos, who is collecting the powerful Infinity Stones. Thanos plans to wipe out half of the universe to restore balance. With the fate of the cosmos hanging in the balance, the Avengers must fight together to prevent disaster, but the stakes are high.

Ant-Man and The Wasp (2018)

Scott Lang, still under house arrest, teams up with Hope van Dyne to rescue her mother, Janet van Dyne, from the Quantum Realm. As they battle a new villain, Ghost, who seeks to control quantum energy, they unlock new dimensions and strengthen their bond as partners and heroes.

Captain Marvel (2019)

Carol Danvers, a Kree warrior, discovers her true Earth origins as she becomes one of the universe’s most powerful heroes. In a cosmic battle against the Skrulls, she uncovers her past, her connection to Earth, and her role in preventing an intergalactic war, embracing her true power.

Avengers: Endgame (2019)

Following the catastrophic events of Infinity War, the remaining Avengers are shattered and mourning their loss. Determined to undo Thanos's snap, they embark on a time-travel mission to recover the Infinity Stones. Along the way, they confront their past, face impossible choices, and make ultimate sacrifices.

Spider-Man: Far From Home (2019)

Peter Parker embarks on a school trip to Europe, but his vacation is interrupted when Mysterio, a hero from another dimension, seeks his help to fight elemental creatures. As Peter struggles with his role as a hero and his loss of Tony Stark, he uncovers Mysterio’s dangerous plans.

Phase 4

Black Widow (2021)

Natasha Romanoff confronts her dark past and estranged family after the events of Civil War. As she goes on the run from Taskmaster, a powerful assassin, she must face the truths of her past and confront her own history as a Russian spy, all while attempting to protect the world.

Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings (2021)

Shang-Chi lives a quiet life until he's pulled back into the shadow of his father's powerful Ten Rings organization. Forced to confront painful memories and ancient threats, he discovers a hidden mystical world tied to his heritage. Embracing his past and unlocking his true potential, Shang-Chi rises as a new kind of Marvel hero.

Eternals (2021)

A group of ancient, immortal beings known as the Eternals has secretly lived on Earth for centuries. When a new cosmic threat arises, they must come together to protect humanity. As they battle their own conflicts, they uncover truths about their past, their purpose, and their impact on Earth.

Spider-Man: No Way Home (2021)

Peter Parker's secret identity is exposed, and his life spirals into chaos. Desperate for a solution, he seeks help from Doctor Strange, but their spell goes wrong, opening the multiverse and bringing villains from alternate realities. Peter must confront them while grappling with the consequences of his actions.

Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness (2022)

Doctor Strange journeys through the multiverse to find a way to stop a multiversal catastrophe. Joined by Wanda Maximoff, they face dangerous threats from alternate realities, uncovering truths about magic, dark powers, and the consequences of tampering with reality itself.

Thor: Love and Thunder (2022)

Thor reunites with Jane Foster, who has mysteriously gained the power of Mjolnir and become the Mighty Thor. Together, they face Gorr the God Butcher, a vengeful being targeting gods across the cosmos. As Thor grapples with love, loss, and legacy, he must rediscover his purpose and protect the universe from a threat fueled by grief.

Black Panther: Wakanda Forever (2022)

Following the death of King T’Challa, Wakanda struggles to find its footing while facing threats from a hidden underwater kingdom led by Namor. Queen Ramonda and Shuri must defend their nation and legacy amid grief, conflict, and change. As a new Black Panther rises, Wakanda must embrace its future while honoring its fallen protector.

Phase 5

Ant-Man and the Wasp: Quantumania (2023)

Scott Lang, Hope, and their family are pulled into the Quantum Realm, a bizarre universe beneath reality. There, they encounter strange creatures, advanced civilizations, and the powerful Kang the Conqueror. As time and space unravel, Scott must make impossible choices to protect his loved ones and stop a villain who threatens the multiverse itself.

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3 (2023)

The Guardians face their most personal mission yet as Rocket's dark past catches up with him. With their found family at stake, they embark on a high stakes rescue against the twisted High Evolutionary. As emotional truths surface and bonds are tested, the team must fight not just for the galaxy but for each other.

The Marvels (2023)

Carol Danvers, Kamala Khan, and Monica Rambeau find their powers mysteriously entangled, forcing them to swap places across the cosmos. As they unite to stop a Kree revolutionary from tearing the universe apart, they must overcome tension, form trust, and harness their combined strength. Together, they become a powerful trio fighting to restore balance across galaxies.

Deadpool & Wolverine (2024)

Deadpool and Wolverine are thrust into an unexpected partnership when a new threat emerges, endangering the multiverse. As the two antiheroes clash with enemies across dimensions, their chaotic personalities and brutal fighting styles create a volatile mix. Together, they must navigate a world of shifting realities, all while questioning whether they’re truly heroes—or just troublemakers.

Captain America: Brave New World (2025)

Sam Wilson steps into his role as the new Captain America, facing a world divided by fear and uncertainty. As global tensions rise, he uncovers political conspiracies and threats from powerful enemies. Battling doubt and danger, Sam must redefine what it means to carry the shield and inspire a new era of justice and unity.

Thunderbolts (2025)

A team of antiheroes and former villains including Yelena Belova, Bucky Barnes, Red Guardian, and U.S. Agent is assembled for a covert government mission. Bound by uneasy alliances and hidden agendas, the Thunderbolts must confront a dangerous threat that could spiral out of control. In a world without Avengers, they may be the only line of defense flawed, but fierce.
